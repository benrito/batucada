Turn Your News Tech Idea Into Reality

  • Participating in the Knight-Mozilla news innovation challenges will net you new collaborators and support.

  • The 60 best contributors will rub shoulders with the Web's leading lights: folks like Christian Heilmann, Burt Herman, Aza Raskin, John Resig, and many more.

Become a Knight-Mozilla Fellow

Five people will become Knight-Mozilla News Technology Fellows in 2011. Another ten will join in 2012.

  • Fellowships are paid, one-year positions with news organizations include Al Jazeera, BBC, Boston.com, the Guardian and Zeit Online.

  • Fellows will work alongside the newsroom staff at their host institutions, and the MoJo community of innovators, to build world-class news tech implementations.
Anyone who participates in an innovation challenge is eligible to become a fellow. What's your idea?

MoJo is an opportunity to get your byline on the future of news. Whether you are a designer, developer, student or entrepreneur, we want to help develop your best ideas about journalism's future. Get involved today and follow us on Twitter.


Host a Knight-Mozilla Fellow

We're looking for news partners with a strong commitment to outstanding journalism and technical innovation to host our 2012 fellows.

Fellows are exceptional news technologists, selected and supported by Knight and Mozilla to advance their ideas inside leading newsrooms. Institutions gain access to a full-time technologist and a broad community of open source innovators. To learn more, please be in touch.
